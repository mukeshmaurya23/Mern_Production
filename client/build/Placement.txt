This web application has been specially developed for job seekers to gain an idea about how an comapany recruiters a suitable candidate for an job position and also gives the insight about the company for which they are preparing for the interview. 
This web application bridges the gap between the job seekers and the recruiters by providing the information about the company and the job position for which they are preparing for the interview.recruiters can also post the job position and the job seekers can apply for the job position.
  This Web application also helps in resolving the issue of not only the job seekers but also the recruiters by providing them the required information about the suitable candidate they have searching for by providing the information about the overall performance of the candidate in the interview.
